WHY SHOULD YOU COME TO UZBEKISTAN?
"Tashkent's modern skyline is rapidly evolving with new skyscrapers, prominently featuring the striking Nest One tower. These towering structures symbolize Uzbekistan's urban modernization and future aspirations."
"Ichan Qala, Khiva's walled inner city, is a remarkably preserved UNESCO site with over 50 historic monuments. It offers a living museum experience, transporting visitors directly into a medieval Silk Road city. "
"Bukhara is home to an extraordinary collection of medieval Islamic monuments, including the majestic Po-i-Kalyan complex. These architectural wonders beautifully showcase centuries of Central Asian Islamic art and scholarship."
"Uzbekistan is one of only two "doubly landlocked" countries in the world, meaning it is entirely surrounded by landlocked countries. This unique geographical position means to reach an ocean, one must cross at least two national borders."
